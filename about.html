Bryan Stites

I’m a risk management and analytics leader with a background in cybersecurity, fintech systems, and data automation. With a deep interest in equity and access, I created SeamCoin to support collaborative payments among diverse groups—including those without access to traditional banking apps.

I believe cryptocurrency and smart contracts can be more than speculative assets—they can be tools for social empowerment and inclusion.
