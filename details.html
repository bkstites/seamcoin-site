Economic Overview

SeamCoin operates on a transparent smart contract framework. Each user’s contribution is logged on-chain, and transactions only proceed when the group-defined threshold is met—such as unanimous or majority confirmation.

Key Features

  • Equitable pooling of crypto funds for group purchases
  • Automatic fund release based on team confirmations
  • Transaction immutability and audit-friendly logging
  • Ethereum-compatible deployment
  • Flexible custodianship (rotating/elected)

Inclusion First

SeamCoin is designed to include users who may lack access to traditional fintech platforms. It supports fair contributions from all members regardless of banking status.
