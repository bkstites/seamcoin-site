Economic Overview

SeamCoin operates on a transparent smart contract framework. Each user's contribution is visible and logged on-chain. Transactions only proceed when the group-defined threshold is met—either 100% participation or majority vote.

Key Features

  • Equitable pooling of crypto funds for group purchases
  • Automatic fund release based on team confirmations
  • Transaction immutability and audit-friendly logging
  • Compatible with Ethereum and EVM-based chains
  • Supports rotating or elected custodians

Inclusion First

SeamCoin is ideal for mixed-access teams—some with banking, some without. It supports simplified onboarding for inclusive financial participation.
